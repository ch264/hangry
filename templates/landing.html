Welcome

Welcome to Hangry!

Hangry was created by a team of hungry and angry individuals who struggled to find perfect recipes in their busy schedules. Join your fellow hangry eaters and find recipes that will calm your hanger! Or share a recipe or two that helped you satisfy your hanger in a crucial moment. Feel free to browse recipes created by the community and save them to your favorites! Just sign up for an account to get started or login if you already have an account.

Meet The Team

What is Hangry?

Hangry (han-gree) adj.

a state of emotional turmoil caused by extreme hunger; a lack of food resulting in a negative change of personality and possible angry outbursts.

Our focus is to give simple and thoughtful recipes. Join us and celebrate the shared love for food.
